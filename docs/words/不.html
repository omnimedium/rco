CHINESE WORD

不

A test article about the word 吧

HSK Level: 2

不 (bù) serves as a negator in Chinese. We can put it before a verb, an adjective, an adverb or a preposition. Sentences with 不 (bù) are used to describe a present, future or habitual event, unless a specific time in the past is stated.
　

不 to negate verbs ##

We can use 不 (bù) to negate an action verb, stative verb or a modal verb.
不 + verb
我不吃冰淇淋。
wǒ bù chī bīngqílín 。
I don't eat ice-cream.
我不是老師。
wǒ bù shì lǎoshī 。
I am not a teacher.
我不會原諒你。
wǒ bù huì yuánliàng nǐ 。
I will not forgive you.
Note that we use another negator 沒 (méi) to negate the verb 有 (yǒu). For example:
我沒有錢。
wǒ méi yǒu qián 。
I don't have money.
我沒有孩子。
wǒ méi yǒu háizi 。
I don't have children.
To learn more about the usage of 有 (yǒu), please click here.

不 to negate adjectives ##

不 + adjective
我的房子不大。
wǒ de fángzi bù dà 。
My house is not big.
他不帥，但很聰明。
tā bù shuài ， dàn hěn cōngmíng 。
He is not handsome but very smart.
這孩子不怪，你才怪。
zhè háizi bù guài ， nǐ cái guài 。
This child is not weird, you are the weird one.

不 to negate adverbs ##

Most of the time we put adverbs before 不 (bù), but there are a few adverbs that can occur before 不 (bù) as well. For example:
不 + adverb
我不常去圖書館。
wǒ bù cháng qù túshūguǎn 。
I don't go to the library often.
你不一定會贏。
nǐ bù yīdìng huì yíng 。
You may not win.

不 to negate adverbs ##

不 + preposition
我今天不在家吃飯。
wǒ jīntiān bù zài jiā chīfàn 。
I am not going to eat at home today.
他現在不跟我說話了。
tā xiànzài bù gēn wǒ shuōhuà le 。
He doesn't talk to me now.
他不與其他人互動，只自己坐在那兒。
tā bù yǔ qítā rén hùdòng ， zhǐ zìjǐ zuò zài nàr 。
He doesn't interact with others, but just sitting there alone.
