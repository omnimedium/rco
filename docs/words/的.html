的

A test article about the word 的

HSK Level: 1     Tags: grammar

的 is one of the most frequent words in Chinese. It has two major functions: adjectivizing and nominalizing, meaning that 的 (de) can make a sentence constituent function like an adjective or a noun.

的 as an adjectivizing particle

It serves to connect a noun/noun phrase with its modifier. Modifiers come in all types and forms, which could be nouns, pronouns, verbs and adjectives, etc. By adding 的 (de) after, we make them function like an adjective which is usually put before a noun. In other words, 的 'adjectivizes' modifiers, which makes them 'ready' to modify their nouns.

We simply put 的 between a modifier and its noun like in the following:

modifier + 的 + head noun
